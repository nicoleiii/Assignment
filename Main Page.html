ABOUT ME

I am a 3D Modeller with three years of experience that specializes in creating digital environments and character models. I am skilled in multiple software platforms, with Blender being the primary tool used for 3D Modelling. I am able to transform ideas into fully functional models that can be used for personal use.

Additionally, I am a Unity scene builder that is capable of constructing worlds that are fully functional for VRChat. Using Unity, I am able to create unique environments that are optimized and fully functional for Virtual Reality users and Desktop users.

PROJECTS

I have had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of communities and clients. I have created original characters and scenes that bring my clients ideas to life. I have created multiple environments with each having their own unique genre. This includes, fantasy, steampunk, cyberpunk, medieval and more.

Each project I create meets the demands of my clients, delivering high-quality, original content that stands out in any genre or setting.
